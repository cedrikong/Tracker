What is chargeblast

Chargeblast is designed to help merchants manage and reduce their dispute rates with advanced transaction data matching from all major card networks. Here's a simple breakdown:

  1. When a customer calls their bank to file a chargeback, Chargeblast detects this activity.
  2. Chargeblast then notifies the business immediately.
  3. This alert is received before the chargeback officially hits your payment processor, allowing the business to take action proactively. This process helps in maintaining a dispute rate of 0% and ensures processing safety for your Shopify store. If you have any more questions, feel free to ask!

    General Rules

    RDR and CDRN actually serve different purposes. If a Visa dispute falls outside of your specific RDR ruleset, the system will attempt to route it as a CDRN alert for manual review. To ensure your dispute ratio is fully protected against gaps in RDR coverage, I highly recommend keeping CDRN active. Ethoca and CDRN together provides up to 70% coverage for the alerts market. By keeping both active alongside RDR, you ensure that your dispute ratio is fully protected across all major card brands, including those that RDR doesn't cover. I highly recommend maintaining this setup to avoid any gaps in your protection.
